## Logo

### Primary Logo
The Mikira logo consists of two elements:
1. **Logo Mark**: A sage green circle (32x32px)
2. **Wordmark**: "MIKIRA" in white, uppercase, bold sans-serif font

### Logo Spacing
- Minimum clear space around logo: 12px
- Logo mark and wordmark gap: 12px

### Logo Usage
- Always maintain the logo mark + wordmark pairing
- Never modify the colors
- Maintain aspect ratios when scaling

## Typography

### Headings
- Font weight: 600-700
- Letter spacing: 0.08em for logo, normal for body headings
- Text transform: Uppercase for logo and small labels

### Body Text
- Font family: "Inter", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif
- Line height: 1.6
- Color: White (#FFFFFF) or muted white for secondary content

## Design Elements

### Decorative Accents
- **Vertical accent bar**: 4px wide gradient from sage green to transparent
- **Horizontal accent bar**: 1px sage green highlight on borders
- **Circle elements**: Sage green circles with varying opacity (0.3-0.6)

### Borders & Shadows
- Gallery items: 1px sage green border with subtle glow
- Box shadows: Use sage green with low opacity (0.1-0.2)
- Border radius: 18-20px for cards, 12px for small elements, 50% for circles

### Interactive Elements
- Hover transitions: 0.2s ease
- Transform on hover: translateY(-4px) for cards
- Background transitions: From accent-soft to accent-hover

## Component Styling

### Header
- Sage green logo mark with white wordmark
- Maintains visibility against black background
- Minimal, clean design

### Gallery Items
- Rounded corners (18px)
- Sage green border (1px)
- Hover effect: Lift and glow
- Maintains photo aspect ratios

### Footer
- Sage green accent line (100px width)
- Instagram icon in brand colors
- Subtle border separator

### Lightbox/Modal
- Black background with backdrop blur
- Sage green border accent
- Navigation buttons styled with brand colors

## Favicon
- Black background with rounded corners (12px radius)
- White "M" letter (36px, bold)
- Sage green circle backgrounds and border rings
- Size: 64x64px SVG

## Accessibility
- High contrast ratio between white text and black background (21:1)
- Sage green provides sufficient contrast for decorative elements
- Focus states use sage green outlines
- All interactive elements have proper ARIA labels

## File Assets
- `assets/icons/favicon.svg` - Favicon/app icon
- `assets/icons/icon.svg` - Logo icon variant
- `assets/icons/instagram.svg` - Branded Instagram icon

## Best Practices
1. Maintain consistency across all pages
2. Use sage green sparingly as an accent, not as a primary element
3. Ensure text remains white on black for maximum readability
4. Keep decorative elements subtle and elegant
5. Test all changes on multiple screen sizes
6. Maintain smooth transitions and animations
